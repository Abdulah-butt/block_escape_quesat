1. Information We Collect

Block Escape Quest is designed to work with minimal data collection. At present, the app:

  • Stores game progress locally on your device.
  • Stores settings such as sound, music, and vibration preferences locally on your device.
  • May access app store metadata for update checks when you use the optional update prompt.
  • May use system sharing features when you choose to share the app with friends.

2. How We Use Information

Any information handled by the app is used only to provide core game functionality, including:

  • Saving and restoring your progress.
  • Remembering your settings.
  • Showing update prompts when a newer app version is available.
  • Supporting optional sharing actions you start manually.

3. Data Storage

Progress and settings are stored locally on the device using standard platform storage. If you uninstall the app or clear its data, local progress may be removed.

4. Third-Party Services

The current build does not depend on advertising for gameplay. If future versions add third-party services such as analytics, ads, or in-app purchases, this policy should be updated to reflect those services and any related data handling.

5. Children’s Privacy

Block Escape Quest is intended to be family-friendly. We do not knowingly request unnecessary personal information from children. If a future feature changes this, the policy should be updated to match the relevant legal and store requirements.

6. Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new “Last Updated” date.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at contact@kodingklouds.com.
